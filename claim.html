How Payouts Work.

Two payout moments across the tournament: the end of the group stage, and the final whistle of the World Cup Final. This page explains exactly how payouts work and how to make sure your wallet qualifies.

ℹ️

How payouts work: At each payout moment — end of group stage and the final whistle — Mundial takes an on-chain snapshot of token holders. Pool SOL is distributed from the Mundial treasury (a Squads multisig) directly to qualifying holder wallets. A 1% platform fee applies. Payout timing is announced on X — follow @Mundial2026_sol so you don't miss the details for your nation.

How to Qualify
Hold a top-3 nation's token at the final whistle of the World Cup Final.

Winner's holders split 70% of the pool, runner-up 20%, third place 10% — each by your share of that nation's holdings at snapshot.

Plus: a $MUNDIAL airdrop from the treasury.

How the Champion Pool Works

01

Buy Any Token

0.25% of every trade on every one of the 48 country tokens flows into the champion pool automatically via creator rewards.

02

Hold to the Final

The pool builds for 32 days. The longer the tournament runs and the more volume there is, the larger the pool grows.

03

Final Whistle Snapshot

At full time of the final, a snapshot captures holders of the top 3 nations' tokens. Your holdings at that moment determine your share of the 70/20/10 split.

04

Receive Your SOL

After the final, a snapshot captures top-3 holders. Payouts are sent directly from the treasury to qualifying wallets within 24 hours. Nothing to claim.

How Payouts Work

01

Hold Tokens

Buy any country token through Mundial. Your holdings are tracked continuously — no registration needed.

02

Match Resolves

After the group stage ends, final group standings are confirmed. Each group pool splits by finish position: 1st 50%, 2nd 25%, 3rd 15%, 4th burns to $MUNDIAL.

03

Claim Opens

After the group stage ends, payouts are calculated from a holder snapshot and distributed directly to qualifying wallets from the treasury. No action needed on your part.

04

Sign & Receive

Payouts are sent directly from the Mundial treasury to qualifying wallets. Holders receive SOL automatically — no signature or claim step required.
