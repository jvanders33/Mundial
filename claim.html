How You
Get Paid.

No "claim now" button that quietly does nothing. When a pool settles, we publish the holder snapshot and a Merkle root on-chain — then you claim your own share against it. Here's exactly how, and exactly when.

The mechanism

Four Steps. All Verifiable.

You never have to trust that we'll "send it." The snapshot is public, the root is on-chain, and the claim is something you trigger yourself.

01

Snapshot

When a pool settles, we record every holder of the qualifying nation tokens at a fixed block. The full snapshot is published so anyone can check their balance is in it.

02

Merkle Root

The snapshot is hashed into a Merkle tree and the root is committed on-chain. Anyone can rebuild the tree from the public snapshot and confirm the root matches.

03

You Claim

The pool is funded into an audited distributor. You connect your wallet here, submit your proof, and pull your share. We never push funds to you — you take them.

04

SOL Arrives

Your share lands directly in the wallet that held the token. One on-chain transaction you can see on Solscan. Done.

Two payout moments — that's it

When Claims Open

There are no knockout-round pools. Funds collect across the whole tournament and pay out at two moments only.

Window 1

Group-Stage Pools

Each group pool pays its holders by final standing — 1st 50%, 2nd 25%, 3rd 15%, and 4th's 10% buys back and burns $MUNDIAL. Opens once the group stage settles.

Window 2

Champion Pool

Built across all 48 tokens for the whole tournament, paid at the Final — 70% to winner holders, 20% runner-up, 10% third place.

What You Need To Qualify

✓Hold the nation's token in a wallet you control (Phantom, Solflare, etc.) through the snapshot block. Self-custody — your keys.
✓Don't leave it on a centralised exchange. If your tokens sit in an exchange account, the snapshot sees the exchange's wallet, not you — and you won't be able to claim.
✓Watch @Mundial2026_sol for the exact snapshot block and the claim window. Both are announced before they happen.

⚠ Protect Yourself

  • We will never DM you first, and we will never ask for your seed phrase or private key. Nobody legitimate ever does.
  • Claims happen only at mundial.gg/claim.html — not via DMs, not via links in replies, not on look-alike domains. Check the URL.
  • There is no "pre-claim," no "verification fee," no "unlock payment." Claiming costs only the normal Solana network fee, paid by you, to your own transaction.
