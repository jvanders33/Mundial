How It Works

01
🪙

Buy Your Nation

Every nation has a token using the official 3-letter FIFA code — ARG, BRA, ENG, AUS. The flag is the token identity. Every trade pays a creator reward to the Mundial treasury, split 50% to the group pool, 25% to the champion pool, 25% to platform & ops.

02
📊

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool fed by the group's half of every creator reward. 1st takes 50%, 2nd 25%, 3rd 15%. 4th place share is used to buy back & burn $MUNDIAL when group standings settle (12+ burn events across the tournament).

03
🏆

Champion Pool

A quarter of every creator reward across all 48 tokens builds the Champion Pool across 32 days. At the final whistle it pays out — 70% to the World Cup winner holders, 20% runner-up, 10% third place.

04
💸

Get Paid in SOL

Payouts happen at two moments: end of the group stage and the final whistle. We publish a holder snapshot and a Merkle root, then you claim your share yourself — see Claim. Follow @Mundial2026_sol for timing.
