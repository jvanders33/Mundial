How It Works

Simple.
On-chain.
Ruthless.

01
🪙

Buy Your Nation

Every one of the 48 World Cup nations has a token on pump.fun. Buy through Mundial to qualify for pool payouts. Early buyers get the cheapest entry on the bonding curve.

02
📊

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool. 2% of every purchase through Mundial feeds the pool. 1st takes 45%, 2nd takes 30%, 3rd takes 10%. 4th place rolls to $MUNDIAL.

03
⚔️

Knockout Match Pools

From the Round of 32 onwards, every match has its own pool. Trade right up to kickoff. Winners claim the full pool. Losers' unclaimed share goes to $MUNDIAL within 48 hours.

04
🏆

Champion Bonus

The 0.5% Champion Pool accumulates across all 48 tokens for 32 days. At full time of the final, it all goes to the winning nation's holders — plus a $MUNDIAL airdrop.
