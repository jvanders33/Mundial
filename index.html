Buy Your Nation

Every nation has a token on pump.fun identified by its official 3-letter FIFA code — ARG, BRA, ENG, AUS. Buy through Mundial to qualify for pool payouts. Early buyers get the cheapest bonding curve price.

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool. 0.8% of every purchase through Mundial feeds it. 1st takes 45%, 2nd 30%, 3rd 10%. 4th place gets nothing — their share rolls to $MUNDIAL.

03
⚔️

Knockout Match Pools

From Round of 32 onwards, every match has its own pool. Trade right up to kickoff. Winners claim the full pool. Losers' unclaimed share goes to $MUNDIAL treasury within 48 hours.

04
🏆

Champion Bonus

The Champion Pool builds across all 48 tokens for 32 days. At the final whistle, it all pays to the winning nation's holders — plus a $MUNDIAL airdrop on top.
