Buy Your Nation

Every nation has a token using the official 3-letter FIFA code — ARG, BRA, ENG, AUS. The flag is the token identity. 1% creator reward is set at launch on pump.fun — 0.5% funds the group pool, 0.25% the champion pool, 0.25% the platform.

02
📊

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool fed by 0.5% of every trade on any platform. 1st takes 50%, 2nd 25%, 3rd 15%. 4th place rolls to $MUNDIAL buyback & burn.

03
⚔️

Knockout Match Pools

From Round of 32 onwards, every match has its own pool. Trade right up to kickoff. Winners claim 100% of the combined pool.

04
🏆

Champion Pool

0.25% of every trade across all 48 tokens builds the Champion Pool for 32 days. At the final whistle it pays out to the top 3 nations — 70% to the winner, 20% to the runner-up, 10% to 3rd place.
