How It Works

Simple.
On-chain.
Ruthless.

01
🪙

Buy Your Nation

Every one of the 48 World Cup nations has a token on pump.fun, identified by its 3-letter FIFA code and flag. Buy $BRA, $ARG, $ENG — whoever you believe in. Early buyers get cheaper entry on the bonding curve.

02
📊

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool. Token purchases feed the pool. At the end of the group stage, it pays out by finishing position — 1st takes the lion's share. 4th place gets nothing, their share rolls to $MUNDIAL.

03
⚔️

Knockout Match Pools

From the Round of 32 onwards, every match has its own pool. Buy before kickoff, trade right up to the whistle. Winners claim the full combined pool. Losers' unclaimed tokens go to the $MUNDIAL treasury within 48 hours.

04
🏆

Champion Bonus

Holders of the winning nation at the final whistle receive their match pool payout plus a $MUNDIAL airdrop from the tournament treasury — rewarding those who backed the right team from the start.
