Buy Your Nation

Every nation has a token using the official 3-letter FIFA code — ARG, BRA, ENG, AUS. The flag is the token identity. Buy through Mundial to qualify for pool payouts.

02
📊

Group Stage Pools

All 4 teams in each group share a prize pool fed by 0.8% of every purchase. 1st takes 45%, 2nd 30%, 3rd 10%. 4th place rolls to $MUNDIAL.

03
⚔️

Knockout Match Pools

From Round of 32 onwards, every match has its own pool. Trade right up to kickoff. Winners claim 100% of the combined pool.

04
🏆

Champion Bonus

0.5% of all purchases across all 48 tokens builds the Champion Pool for 32 days. At full time of the final — it all goes to the winning nation's holders.
